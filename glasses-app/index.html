Status and controls are on the glasses. Configure the backend and car preferences here; they are saved to the Even app.

Setup guide

This app needs a small self-hosted backend that talks to Genesis Connected Services — your credentials stay on your server, never in this app.

  1. Open the project's GitHub page (link below) and click Deploy to Render.
  2. Enter your Genesis username, password and PIN when Render asks.
  3. If your Genesis account isn't registered in the EU, set CONNECT_REMOTE_REGION in Render's Environment tab to match its region.
  4. After the first deploy, copy the service's HTTPS URL and the generated CONNECT_REMOTE_API_TOKEN from Render's Environment tab.
  5. Paste both below, then hit Test connection. First contact can take a minute while the free tier wakes.
