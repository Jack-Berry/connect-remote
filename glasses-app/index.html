Genesis Remote settings

Status and controls are on the glasses. Sign in to your car account and set preferences here; they are saved to the Even app on this phone.

Setup guide

Sign in with the same Genesis Connected Services account you use in the official Genesis app. Your details are stored only on this phone (in the Even app) and sent, encrypted, straight to the app's relay when you use a control; the relay keeps nothing.

  1. Enter your Genesis Connected Services username and password below.
  2. Enter your PIN, the code the official app asks for before lock/unlock. Most accounts have one.
  3. Pick the region your account is registered in.
  4. Hit Test connection. The first sign-in can take up to a minute.
  5. Save. Status and controls live on the glasses.
